Our Vision

Our vision is to promote social well-being within the community, helping
those most impacted by the pandemic to achieve self-reliance sustainably, allowing them to lead lives with dignity.

Who We Are

We are a team of dedicated and passionate team members of Hamara Sehyog NGO based in Pune, dedicated to implementing social and welfare programs aimed at providing social security for vulnerable, impoverished, and underprivileged communities, particularly those impacted by COVID-19. A substantial number of individuals reside in slums and bastis across cities and towns, often at the lowest tier of the economic pyramid. Many of these individuals lack access to regular food and shelter, relying on scarce resources to survive in makeshift temporary homes constructed from available materials. The pandemic has exacerbated their struggles, leaving them among the hardest hit. We are a small group of individuals who are relatively better off and eager to assist using our own resources, supplemented by donations from those willing to support our cause. We consider it a privilege to extend our help to this small segment of the population.
